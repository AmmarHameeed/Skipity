Privacy Policy for Skipity

Last updated: 11th February 2025

Skipity ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Extension handles your information.


1. Data Collection

The Extension does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data. It operates locally on your device and does not transmit any information to external servers.


2. Permissions

The Extension requires access to Netflix (*://*.netflix.com/*) & Youtube (*://*.youtube.com/*) to detect and automatically click the "Skip Intro," "Skip Recap," and "Skip Ad" buttons. It does not access your Netflix account information, viewing history, or any other data.


3. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
